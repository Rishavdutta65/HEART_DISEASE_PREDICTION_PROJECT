About the Attributes

These are the medical attributes used for heart disease prediction.

1. Age - Age of the patient (Min: 29.0, Max: 77.0)

2. Sex - Male (1) / Female (0)

3. cp - Chest pain type (0-3)

4. trestbps - Resting blood pressure (Min: 94.0, Max: 200.0)

5. chol - Serum cholesterol in mg/dl (Min: 126.0, Max: 564.0)

6. fbs - Fasting blood sugar > 120 mg/dl (1=True; 0=False)

7. restecg - Resting ECG results (0-2)

8. thalach - Maximum heart rate achieved (Min: 71.0, Max: 202.0)

9. exang - Exercise induced angina (1=Yes; 0=No)

10. oldpeak - ST depression induced by exercise (Min: 0.0, Max: 6.2)

11. slope - Slope of peak exercise ST segment (0-2)

12. ca - Number of major vessels colored by fluoroscopy (0-3)

13. thal - Thalassemia (1=Fixed; 2=Normal; 3=Reversible)
